Frequently asked questions

If you can't find what you are looking for don't hesitate to contact us.

What is RecipeMatch?

RecipeMatch is an intelligent recipe discovery platform that helps you find perfect recipes based on the ingredients you already have at home.

How does RecipeMatch work?

Simply input the ingredients you have available, and our smart algorithm will suggest recipes that match your ingredients, dietary preferences, and cooking time.

Do I need to create an account to use the app?

You can browse recipes without an account, but creating one allows you to save favorites, track your cooking history, and get personalized recommendations.

Can I save my favorite recipes?

Yes! With a free account, you can save unlimited recipes, create custom collections, and access them anytime across all your devices.

What types of cuisines are available in RecipeMatch?

We offer recipes from cuisines worldwide, including Italian, Asian, Mexican, Mediterranean, American, and many more, with new recipes added regularly.

Can I search for recipes by ingredient?

Absolutely! Our ingredient-based search is our core feature. You can search by single ingredients or combinations to find the perfect recipe.

Are the recipes beginner-friendly?

Yes! We have recipes for all skill levels, with clear difficulty ratings, step-by-step instructions, and helpful cooking tips for beginners.
